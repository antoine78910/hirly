Outcome

user.define_outcome · max_iterations 3

Your definition of done — rubric.content, graded by the platform every run.

  1. Every material application claim has at least one valid candidate-evidence reference
  2. No unsupported, uncertain, or conflicting claim is presented as fact
  3. Salary, work authorization, relocation, legal attestations, and demographic answers are left for the candidate unless explicitly provided
  4. The draft is specific to the supplied job, not generic
  5. Job requirements and draft claims remain traceable to their sources
  6. The output never claims a submission, callback, interview, offer, or job outcome occurred
